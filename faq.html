Frequently asked questions

Unlock your physical potential

Do you take insurance?

We do not take insurance as it limits the areas we can focus on and we aim to address your whole body.

How can you evaluate me through the computer?

Great question! We have a series of movements that we observe to assess mobility, balance, and functional strength. Based on what we see, coupled with your medical history and symptoms, we are able to address underlying causes and create an effective program.

How do I download the new exercise program?

We set up your profile on our app and stream it to review it together in session!

How do I schedule an appointment?

We recommend starting with a free phone consultation to see if EnPhysion will be a good fit. You can book your call here: https://calendly.com/enphysion/15min

How is this different than in person physical therapy?

We focus on your whole body
We address habit formation to ensure you perform your program
We are able to hold you accountable and reach out between sessions if needed
Our session lengths vary depending on what an individual needs
It is provided virtually
There is no doctors referral required

How is this different than personal training?

You are evaluated by a Doctor of Physical Therapy
We hold you accountable between sessions
We charge one affordable fee for multiple sessions
We leverage technology to deliver a safe and effective movement/exercise program to your phone or tablet

What is the cost?

We have multiple options, but our most common membership is just $99/month

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancel anytime!

Do you take Medicare?

We do not take Medicare at this time and cannot provide Physical Therapy to Medicare enrollees. We can provide personal training to focus on strength, balance and flexibility. We are happy to refer you to an in person clinic that accepts Medicare.


Where are you located?

We are a virtual company and do not have a physical office for clients. We can offer in person evaluations by our Doctors of Physical therapy if you are located in the Boston area performed at your home.
